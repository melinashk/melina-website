Introduction

Ramailo Shopping is a website whereby a customer gets points whenever they shop from a supermarket. The customer gets points according to the amount they shop. The points are added up and they also get gift hampers after certain points is attained. There are different gift hampers according to the points they earn.A customer may or may not take the gift for certain point and keep on adding the points on their accounts.

Technologies Used

Qt, C++
