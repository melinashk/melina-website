Introduction

Mohan Biscuit Factory is a website for the famous biscuit factory located at Dhankuta. The website facilitates the customer to view the product details, know about the factory, make inquiries and also apply for job. The other part of the whole project includes the software that will be installed in the local system. This includes updating stocks and products and the facility of billing system. The project is in progress.

Technologies Used

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, Django and PostgreSQL
